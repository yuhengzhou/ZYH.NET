General English Test – Level A2

This test measures your ability to understand and use English. The test has four sections.

You have a total of 125 minutes to complete the test. The time to read all the direction pages is included in the total time allowed for the test.

At the beginning of each section, there are directions that explain how to answer the questions or respond to the tasks in the section. You must answer every question in each section.

In the Listening section, you will have 25 questions to answer. We suggest spending 40 minutes to finish this section.

In the Reading section, you will have 35 questions to answer. We suggest spending 40 minutes to finish this section.

In the Speaking section, there are 6 tasks. It will take about 10 minutes to finish this section.

In the Writing section, there is one task. We suggest spending up to 30 minutes to finish this section.


You will be able to review the questions you have completed and be able to return to any question, where review is allowed, as long as there is still time available for that question or the test. You can use the Review button to navigate to the questions in the test. With the Review pop-up window open, you can see which questions you have answered and which you have not. Click the Go button to the right of a question to go to that question. You cannot jump to questions you have not viewed yet. The Review pop-up window also shows which questions you flagged. To flag a question, as a reminder to come back to it later, click the flag icon that is seen to the right of the test name in the upper left area of your testing page. When you click on the icon the first time, the flag changes from grey (inactive) to red (active) . To unflag a question, click on the icon once more and it will change back to its inactive, grey, state.

To go to the next question from the one you are viewing, click on the Next button . To go to the previous question, click on the Back button .


When you come to the last question, the Next button will disappear and be replaced with a Submit button. If there is still time remaining in the test, you may use it to review your answers by using the Review button. When you are ready to end the test, click the Submit button . You will see the Test Complete page and then you can go back to your Home Page or Logout.

If necessary, you can stop the test early at any time by clicking the End Test button . This will submit your test. It is not possible to return to the test once End Test has been clicked.
